# Marketing Strategy - AutoToko

## 🎯 Target Market

### Primary Target:
**UMKM/Toko Online Skala Kecil-Menengah**

**Karakteristik:**
- Omzet: Rp 10jt - 500jt/bulan
- Order: 10-200 per hari
- Channel: WhatsApp, Instagram, Marketplace
- Pain: Manual tasks, time-consuming, sulit scale
- Budget: Rp 1-5jt untuk automation

**Segmentasi:**

1. **Fashion & Apparel** (30%)
   - Baju, sepatu, aksesoris
   - High volume, repetitive tasks
   - Butuh inventory management

2. **Food & Beverage** (25%)
   - Kue, catering, frozen food
   - Fast response needed
   - Inventory tracking penting

3. **Beauty & Cosmetics** (20%)
   - Skincare, makeup, parfum
   - Banyak pertanyaan produk
   - Butuh professional image

4. **Handmade & Craft** (15%)
   - Custom products
   - Personal touch penting
   - Butuh efficient order management

5. **Others** (10%)
   - Electronics, books, toys, dll

## 💰 Pricing & Promotion Strategy

**Early Bird Special:**
- 10 client pertama: **Diskon 30%**
- Gratis maintenance 2 bulan
- Bonus: 1x free customization

**Urgency:**
- Limited slots: "Hanya 10 slot tersedia!"
- Countdown timer di landing page
- Social proof: "5 slots sudah terisi!"

**Monthly Promo:**
- Setiap bulan ada promo berbeda

- Diskon 10-20%

**Referral Program:**

**Bundle Deals:**
- 2 paket → Diskon 15%

## 📊 Sales Funnel

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Goal:** Orang tau AutoToko exists

**Tactics:**
- Social media content
- SEO blog posts
- YouTube videos
- Paid ads

**Metrics:** Reach, impressions, website visits

### Stage 2: Interest
**Goal:** Orang tertarik, mau tau lebih lanjut

**Tactics:**
- Lead magnet: "Free Guide: 10 Automation Ideas"
- Free webinar: "Cara Otomasi Toko Online"
- Case studies & testimonials
- Demo video

**Metrics:** Email signups, webinar registrations

### Stage 3: Consideration
**Goal:** Orang compare options, evaluate

**Tactics:**
- Free consultation (30 min)
- Custom proposal
- ROI calculator
- FAQ & objection handling

**Metrics:** Consultation bookings, proposal sent

### Stage 4: Decision
**Goal:** Orang decide to buy

**Tactics:**
- Limited-time offer
- Social proof (testimonials)
- Money-back guarantee
- Easy payment options

**Metrics:** Conversion rate, sales

### Stage 5: Retention
**Goal:** Keep clients happy, upsell

**Tactics:**
- Excellent support
- Regular check-ins
- Upsell: maintenance, add-ons
- Referral program

**Metrics:** Retention rate, LTV, referrals

- Optimize conversion funnel

## 💬 Sales Script

### Initial Contact (WhatsApp/DM)

**You:**
```
Halo kak! Terima kasih sudah tertarik dengan AutoToko 😊

Boleh tau sekarang toko online kakak:
1. Jualan apa?
2. Berapa order per hari?
3. Pakai channel apa? (WA/IG/Marketplace)
4. Apa challenge terbesar saat ini?

Biar saya bisa kasih rekomendasi yang tepat!
```

**After Understanding:**
```
Oke kak, saya paham situasinya.

Berdasarkan info kakak, saya rekomendasikan [PAKET NAME].

Dengan paket ini, kakak bisa:
✅ [Benefit 1]
✅ [Benefit 2]
✅ [Benefit 3]

Hemat waktu [XX] jam/hari!

Mau saya kirim detail lengkap + demo video?
```

### Handling Objections

**"Mahal"**
```
Saya paham kak. Tapi coba hitung:
- Sekarang kakak spend [XX] jam/hari untuk manual tasks
- Nilai waktu kakak: Rp 50k/jam
- Dalam 1 bulan: Rp [XXX]k

Investment Rp [X]jt ini balik modal dalam 1 bulan.
Setelah itu pure saving! 

Plus, kakak bisa fokus ke hal yang lebih penting: 
marketing, product development, dll.

Mau saya buatkan ROI calculation detail?
```

**"Nanti dulu"**
```
Boleh kak, no pressure 😊

Tapi FYI, promo early bird kita cuma sampai [DATE].
Setelah itu harga normal.

Gimana kalau kita schedule free consultation dulu?
30 menit aja, no commitment.

Kakak bisa tanya-tanya, saya bisa kasih insight.
Deal?
```

**"Bisa bikin sendiri"**
```
Betul kak, technically bisa!

Tapi pertanyaannya:
1. Berapa lama waktu belajar + setup? (20-40 jam)
2. Berapa opportunity cost-nya?
3. Gimana kalau ada bug/error?

Dengan service kami:
✅ Setup 7 hari
✅ Tested & proven
✅ Support included
✅ Kakak fokus ke bisnis

Time is money, kak 😊

Mau lihat demo dulu?
```
